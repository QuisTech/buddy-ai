# Buddy AI

Buddy AI is a hands-free AI visual tutor that sees, hears, and explains your study materials in real-time.

## Key Features

- **Hands-Free Interaction**: Continuous voice recognition allows for natural conversation without tapping.
- **Visual Intelligence**: Use your camera to show diagrams, text, or problems, and Buddy will explain them.
- **Dynamic Annotations**: Buddy can draw on captured images to highlight key concepts.
- **Adaptive Clarifications**: Receives tailored explanations and analogies based on your specific confusion.
