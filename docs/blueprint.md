# **App Name**: Dynamic Study Buddy

## Core Features:

- Real-time Visual Analysis & Explanations: Utilizes Gemini Vision to 'see' and analyze visual learning materials (e.g., diagrams, graphs, text on a page) in real-time via camera input, providing immediate, context-aware explanations and insights. The AI acts as a tool to interpret complex visual data and explain it in an understandable way.
- Interactive Voice Conversation with Barge-in: Enables natural spoken language interaction, allowing students to ask follow-up questions, request deeper dives, or interrupt the AI agent's responses for a truly conversational learning experience.
- Adaptive Clarification & Analogies: Leverages generative AI to identify areas of student confusion and proactively generate alternative explanations, analogies, or simplified concepts tailored to the student's current understanding, as detected through real-time spoken cues. This feature functions as a tool for personalized tutoring.
- Dynamic Visual Annotations: Allows the AI agent to overlay annotations, highlights, or pointers directly onto the live visual feed of the study material to visually guide the student through explanations of complex parts of diagrams or text.
- Study Session Summaries: Automatically generates a concise text summary of key concepts discussed, problem areas identified, and solutions provided during a study session, aiding in review and retention.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: A thoughtful and focused blue (#1A5FE6) that inspires learning and clarity in a light theme. This evokes intelligence and trustworthiness without being overly formal.
- Background Color: A subtle, desaturated cool gray-blue (#EFF2F4) to create a clean, calm, and distraction-free learning environment, allowing content to stand out.
- Accent Color: A clear and encouraging cyan (#4DAAD4) used for interactive elements, highlights, and positive feedback to draw attention without being intrusive. This color creates a fresh, optimistic feeling.
- Headlines and prominent text will use 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), lending a modern, crisp, and slightly technical feel. Body text and longer explanations will use 'Inter' (sans-serif) for optimal readability and a neutral, objective tone, ensuring comfort during extended study sessions.
- Utilize simple, outline-based icons with rounded corners. Icons should clearly represent actions or concepts (e.g., microphone for input, camera for visual scan, lightbulb for insights), maintaining a friendly and accessible visual language that is intuitive for students.
- Employ a clean, responsive layout with ample white space to reduce cognitive load. Focus on direct visual feedback and clear segmentation for different modes of interaction (e.g., live camera feed area, transcription, and generated responses). Elements should dynamically adjust to present the most relevant information without clutter.
- Incorporate subtle, functional animations such as smooth transitions for content changes, reactive visual feedback to voice input (e.g., waveform visualization), and gentle fades for AI responses appearing on screen. Animations should enhance understanding and responsiveness without distracting from the learning material.
